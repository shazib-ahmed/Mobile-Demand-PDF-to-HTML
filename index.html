MobileDemand mobile point-of-sale solutions:
  1. End long customer lines with a quicker way to pay

KEY ADVANTAGES OF MOBILE POS

Increase Footprint

Eliminate long customer waits with line busting or take your mobile POS to outdoor tents, sidewalks, the sales floor, parking lot events and more. Mobility gives your sales team a larger footprint to engage with more customers

Increase Conversions

Increase sales and personalize service by assisting customers with product information, availability and delivery dates during face-to-face interactions

Ease of Expansion

Mobile POS devices are lower cost than traditional checkout and payment options, allowing faster implementation of additional stores with minimal investment

Easy to Carry and Hold

Fatigue can quickly become a factor during the workday. Our mobile POS devices are equipped with an easy carry briefcase handle and back hand strap that allow a safe, secure and comfortable grip.

MOBILE POS OPTIONS

No two businesses are alike, that is why we offer multiple options to complete your mobile point-of-sale solution. Option 1- Includes the 3-in-1 MagTek tDynamo payment module fully integrated into the lightweight, rugged T1190 tablet. Option 2 - Consists of a combination of rugged devices and multiple module brackets designed to hold a variety of popular POS devices.

Option 1

Magtek tDynamo and T1190 All-in-one fully integrated mobile POS solution

The fully integrated tDynamo payment system is part of our rugged T1190 xTablet. The versatile reader takes conventional plastic cards and mobile wallets

The tDynamo delivers:
  1. Magnetic stripe
  2. Contactless/NFC and EMV chip cards

Option 2

Payment Module Brackets Rugged devices with brackets fitting popular payment modules

You can build your mobile point-of-sale solution from a variety of MobileDemand brackets that support several leading POS modules.

The brackets:
  1. Attach securely to select MobileDemand rugged devices
  2. Rotate 180 degrees allowing customers to enter private data
  3. Choose from several popular Verifone or Ingenico payment modules, sold separately

Protect your Surface tablet with MobileDemand’s Rugged xCase.
